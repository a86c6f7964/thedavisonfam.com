My husband and I live in the wonderful city of Austin, TX. We got married on February 2, 2013 and are learning how to do life as a married couple for the glory of Christ. We love being outside, biking, good beer, great friends, and family. My husband is a software engineer and loves what he does. I am currently a  stay at home wife figuring out what the next step is for me. I love trying to DIY projects, and staying organized. More than any of those things we want to live every moment of life for Christ's glory because we believe that He has saved us from sin and death once and for all.
